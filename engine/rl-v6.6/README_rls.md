RLSwordigo is a mod inspired by the famous minecraft modpack, RLCraft. It offers a "Swordigo-2" experience everyone has wanted. It includes various different things like new npcs, abilities, an inventory system, enchantments and baubles.

Mod Version: 6.6
Mod Author: NeverTrolls
Mod Developers: NeverTrolls, ShadowTheOne, Yeghor Play
